Why Mental Health is Important for Gamers?

Social Isolation is very common among today’s population. This can bring in the “loneliness”. Gaming often helps people bring in a sense of togetherness. Sharing game with other players help relax. Gaming is often taken as a way to cope with stress and a means of distraction. But most of the time gamers might not realise the longer they play longer this makes the opposite impact. Uncontrolled gaming can cause addiction. This can lead to higher anxiety levels and low satisfaction with life. It is crucial for gamers to understand when to refrain from constant gaming.

What is Mental Health or Wellbeing?


Mental health is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realize their abilities, learn well and work well, and contribute to their community. It is an integral component of health and well-being that underpins our individual and collective abilities to make decisions, build relationships and shape the world we live in. Mental health is a basic human right. And it is crucial to personal, community and socio-economic development.

What is Anxiety?


Intense, excessive and persistent worry and fear about everyday situations. It results in fast heart rate, rapid breathing, sweating and feeling tired. Signs and Symptoms of Anxiety include:
Feeling nervous, restless or tense Having a sense of impending danger, panic or doom Having an increased heart rate, Breathing rapidly (hyperventilation), Sweating, Trembling etc.

What is satisfaction with Life?


According to Ruut Veenhoven:
"Life satisfaction is the degree to which a person positively evaluates the overall quality of his/her life as a whole."
In other words, how much the person likes the life he/she leads.” High life satisfaction helps appreciate not only one’s achievements but also small things in life. This indeed improves productivity , coping with stress easier and brings in happiness.
